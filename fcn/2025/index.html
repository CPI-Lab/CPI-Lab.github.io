Welcome!

The 12th Future Computing and Networking (FCN 2025) is an academic event jointly organized by University of Hamburg, TU Delft, University of Helsinki, Tsinghua, TU Munich, and HKUST. The FCN series is intended to bring together professors, researchers and graduate students to share ideas, experience and implementations related to future computing and networking such as Edge AI, Sustainable Computing, and System Networking.

Host

Department of Informatics (Distributed Operating Systems), University of Hamburg

Topics

FCN 2025 will cover following areas:

  • Edge Computing
  • Edge AI
  • Generative AI
  • Future Mobile 6G
  • IoT Systems and Communications
  • Mobile and Networking Services
  • Intelligent Wireless Networks
  • Security and Privacy
  • Approximate Computing
  • Data Analytics

Event Date

  • 25 - 26 September 2025

Social Dinner

  • The FCN event will conclude with a social dinner and an opportunity to network while dining together.
