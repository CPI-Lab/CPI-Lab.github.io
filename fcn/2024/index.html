Welcome!

The 10th Future Computing and Networking workshop on Edge AI, IoT and Networking (FCN 2024) is an academic event jointly organized by Institut für Systems Engineering Leibniz Universität Hannover, TU Delft, University of Helsinki, Tsinghua, TU Munich, and HKUST. The workshop is intended to bring together professors, researchers and graduate students to share ideas, experience and implementations related to edge computing, IoT, cyber-physical systems, distributed systems, networking and data analytics.

Host

Department of Computer Science, University of Helsinki

Topics

The workshop will cover following areas:

  • Edge Computing
  • Edge AI
  • IoT systems and communications
  • IoT services
  • Intelligent wireless network
  • Security and privacy
  • Approximate Computing

Workshop Date

  • Workshop: September 27, 2024
