Welcome!

The 7th Finland-China-Netherlands workshop on mobile networking, analytics and edge intelligence is a three-day workshop jointly organized by TU Delft, University of Helsinki, Tsinghua University, TU Munich, and HKUST. The workshop is intended to bring together professors, researchers and graduate students to share ideas, experience and implementations related to mobile networking, data analytics, edge intelligence and communication.

Topics

The workshop will cover following areas:

  • Intelligent wireless network
  • Intelligent technology and applications
  • Security and privacy
  • Data mining
  • Recommender system
  • Edge intelligence
